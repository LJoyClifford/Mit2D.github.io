It's Pal, the Wonder Dog!

(better known as Pete)

Pal the Wonder Dog was an film actor in the early 1920's. He appeared as Buster Brown's "Tige", and is best known as his portrayal of "Pete" from the Our Gang series. Pal crossed the Rainbow Bridge in 1930. Click me to learn more!
